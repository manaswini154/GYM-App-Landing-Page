// The System

How the AI Coach
Actually Works

01

Position your camera

Face your phone or laptop camera. The system uses MediaPipe to detect 33 body landmarks across your entire skeleton — no wearables needed.

02

Pick your workout plan

Choose your exercise, set your target sets and reps. The AI coach configures itself to track the exact angles and metrics for that movement.

03

Train with live feedback

Reps are counted automatically. Form issues — sagging hips, shallow squats, elbow drift — are caught the moment they happen and spoken aloud.

04

Track your history

Every set, rep, and session is logged. Your workout history aggregates daily so you can see real progress over time.

// Capabilities

Built to Coach
Not Just Count

Most fitness apps count. This one coaches. There's a difference.

👁️

Skeleton Pose Detection

MediaPipe tracks 33 landmarks on your body every frame. Joints, angles, visibility — all computed in real-time directly in your browser.

🎙️

AI Voice Feedback

Powered by LLaMA 3.3 70B via Groq, the coach speaks natural, contextual corrections — not robotic alerts. It knows what exercise you're doing.

📐

Angle-Based Form Analysis

Knee angle, back angle, elbow angle — every movement has mathematically defined thresholds. Bad form is detected before injury happens.

🔁

Automatic Rep Counting

Stage-based rep detection using pose transitions. No button presses — just move naturally and the system tracks every rep and set.

📊

Workout History

SQLite-backed persistence stores your full session history, aggregated daily per exercise. Track reps, sets and time across sessions.

⚡

No-Setup Start

Just enter a username. No account creation, no email, no payment. Your data is yours, stored locally and associated with your name.
